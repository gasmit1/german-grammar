g k
Spelling: g Spelling: k, g
Pronunciation: When preceding a vowel, pronounced same as English "g". Pronounced as ich-sound in the suffix -ig at the end of a word. Pronunciation: Same as English "k", but with less aspiration (release of air). g is hardened to k when it occurs at the end of a syllable, or before an s or t.
