Verbs with separable prefixes

Many German verbs have a prefix in their infinitive form, which separates from the verb in certain sentence structures and appears at the end of the phrase.

List of separable prefix verbs
Erich macht das Fenster auf.
Eric, mach das Fenster auf!
 		Eric is opening the window.
Eric, open the window! .

If the sentence structure requires that the whole verb appear at the end of the phrase (e.g., in dependent clauses), the two parts are unified; if not, they are separated.

List of separable prefix verbs
Eric hat das Fenster aufgemacht.
Eric will das Fenster aufmachen.
Erich sagt, dass er das Fenster aufmacht. 		Eric has opened the window.
Eric wants to open the window.
Erich says that he is opening the window.
