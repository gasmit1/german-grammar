ng The glottal stop
Spelling: ng Spelling: None, shown below as '
Pronunciation: Like the "ng"-sound in English "singer", rather than the "ng"-sound in English "finger". No "g" is included. Pronunciation: Before words or syllables beginning with vowels, slightly stop the flow of air by closing and opening the glottis (the space between the vocal chords).
