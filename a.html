Long a Short a
Spelling: aa, ah, a Spelling: a
Pronunciation: Like the vowel in English "spa", but with mouth wide-open, and no offglide into an "uh"-sound Pronunciation: Between the vowels in English "hut" and "hot". Very short and clipped
