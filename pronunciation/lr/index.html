l and r
l r
Spelling: l, ll Spelling: r, rr
Pronunciation: Much lighter than English "l". Say "million", but touch the tip of the tongue to the ridge behind the upper teeth and flatten it to produce the German l sound. Pronunciation: Before a vowel, pronounce by gargling slightly with uvula at back of mouth. Otherwise "swallow" the r, as in British "here" (hee-uh)
