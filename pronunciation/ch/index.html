ch and ig
ich-sound ach-sound
Spelling: ch, ig Spelling: ch
Pronunciation: Say English "hue", but raise tongue to restrict passage of air for the "h". Used after the "front" vowels (i, e, ä, ö, ü), the diphthongs [ai] and [oi], and the consonants n, l, and r. Pronunciation: Say English "lock", but open throat to allow passage of air for the "ck". Used after the "back" vowels (a, o, u) and the diphthong [au].
