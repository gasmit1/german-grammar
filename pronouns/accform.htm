Personal pronouns - accusative case

The personal pronouns in the accusative case are:

Singular Plural Singular & Plural
mich = me uns = us
dich = you (informal) euch = you (informal plural) Sie = you (formal)
ihn = him, it (masculine)
sie = her, it (feminine)
es = it 		sie = them
