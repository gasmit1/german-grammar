Poems, Songs & Comedy

One of my greatest joys in having learned German is being able to understand German poems, songs and comedy sketches in their original language. To provide you with the same pleasure, I have compiled a list of my favorites, with links to pages which show them, and also with recordings of their performance. This is a fun way to listen to German and let its sounds, words and sentence structure enter your consciousness.
