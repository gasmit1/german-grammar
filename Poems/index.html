Poems and Songs

One of my greatest joys in having learned German is being able to understand German poems and songs in their original language. To provide you with the same pleasure, I have compiled a list of my favorite songs, with links to the poems or lyrics and translations, and also with links to recordings of their performance. This is a fun way to listen to German and let its sounds, words and sentence structure enter your consciousness.
