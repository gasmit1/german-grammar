Questions
Using question words (W-words)

In questions which ask for a specific piece of information, the word order follows the pattern:
Question word - verb - subject - predicate components

Was machen Sie heute abend? What are you doing this evening?

Ja-nein questions

In questions which ask for a yes or no response, the word order follows the pattern:
Verb - subject - predicate components

Gehen Sie in die Stadt? Are you going into the city?
