Expressions with würde

The subjunctive form of werden, in combination with the infinitive of the main verb, is often used instead of the present time subjunctive of the main verb in the following contexts:

  1. when the subjunctive of the main verb looks the same as its simple past tense
  2. to make a polite request, rather than a demand
  3. in the "then" clause of an "if...then" statement expressing a conjecture
  1. Ich würde kein Bier bestellen.
  2. Würdest du mir bitte das Salz reichen?
  3. Wenn ich Sie wäre, würde ich das rote Kleid kaufen.
I wouldn't order a beer
Would you please pass me the salt?
If I were you, I would buy the red dress.
